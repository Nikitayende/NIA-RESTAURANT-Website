About NIA RESTAURANT

Founded in 2024 by Chef Nia, NIA Restaurant is a place where taste meets tradition. We serve a wide variety of delicious vegetarian dishes, including authentic Maharashtrian cuisine and popular Indian favorites.
Our mission is to provide fresh, hygienic, and affordable food while maintaining rich flavors and quality. From homely meals to special dishes, every item is prepared with care and passion.

At NIA Restaurant, we believe in “Atithi Devo Bhava” — treating every guest like family.

Our Values

  • Quality Food: We use fresh ingredients to ensure the best taste and hygiene.
  • Customer First: Every guest is important to us, and we aim to give the best service.
  • Authentic Taste: We preserve traditional flavors, especially Maharashtrian cuisine.
  • Affordable Pricing: Delicious food at prices everyone can enjoy.
  • Cleanliness: Maintaining a clean and safe dining environment is our priority.
  • Passion for Cooking: Every dish is made with love and dedication.

Certifications

  • FSSAI Certified: Licensed and approved by Food Safety and Standards Authority of India.
  • Hygiene & Safety Certified: Maintains high standards of cleanliness and food safety.
  • Quality Food Assurance: Ensuring fresh ingredients and best quality in every dish.
  • Local Food Authority Approved: Registered under local municipal food safety guidelines.

Awards & Recognition

  1. Best Emerging Restaurant 2024: Recognized for outstanding food quality and service.
  2. Hygiene Excellence Award: Awarded for maintaining high standards of cleanliness and safety.
  3. Customer Choice Award: Loved and highly rated by our valued customers.
  4. Authentic Taste Recognition: Appreciated for preserving traditional Maharashtrian flavors.
  5. Top Local Restaurant: Ranked among the best restaurants in the area.

Our Specialities

We are famous for:
  • Signature Maharashtrian Thali: Zunka Bhakar, Pithla, Bhakri, Thecha, and Koshimbir
  • Special Paneer Dishes: Paneer Butter Masala, Kadhai Paneer, Shahi Paneer
  • Traditional Sweets: Puran Poli, Shrikhand, Gulab Jamun
  • Street-Style Favorites: Tawa Pulao, Pav Bhaji style dishes, Chaat-inspired items
  • Healthy Options: Sprouts Salad, Multigrain Thalipeeth, Fresh Juices
  • Refreshing Beverages: Nimbu pani, Kokum Sharbat, Lassi, Fresh Juices
